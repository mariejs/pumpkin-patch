Pumpkin Varieties

Discover our wide variety of pumpkin sizes, shapes, and colors, perfect for all your creative pumpkin projects. From classic orange pumpkins to unique heirloom varieties, we have something for everyone.

Sugar Pie Pumpkin

Taste: The Sugar Pie Pumpkin is known for its sweet and creamy flavor. It is perfect for making pumpkin pies, as it has a smooth and velvety texture when cooked.

You can use a Sugar Pie Pumpkin to make delicious pumpkin pies with its smooth and velvety texture and sweet and creamy flavor. Additionally, you can use it to make pumpkin bread or pumpkin soup for a moist and flavorful treat or a comforting and creamy dish, respectively.

Cinderella Pumpkin

Taste: The Cinderella Pumpkin has a slightly nutty and earthy flavor. It is great for roasting or baking, as it becomes tender and flavorful when cooked.

The Cinderella Pumpkin is perfect for roasting or baking. When cooked, it becomes tender and flavorful, adding a delicious depth to your dishes. Try using Cinderella Pumpkin in recipes like roasted pumpkin soup or pumpkin bread for a delightful autumn treat.

Blue Hubbard Pumpkin

Taste: The Blue Hubbard Pumpkin has a rich and sweet flavor with hints of nuttiness. It is often used in soups and stews, as it adds a delicious depth of flavor.

With a Blue Hubbard Pumpkin, you can prepare a delicious roasted pumpkin soup by scooping out the seeds and roasting the flesh with savory herbs and spices before blending it into a creamy soup.
